ワールドカップ2026の話し方

Session date: 2026-06-22. Level: beginner to lower-intermediate. Focus: natural workplace small talk about Japan's World Cup group-stage situation, modest optimism, and the next Sweden match.

sports small talk workplace conversation soft predictions current event

Topic and Context

Japan are in the 2026 FIFA World Cup group stage. As of this lesson, Japan had drawn 2-2 with the Netherlands and beaten Tunisia 4-0. The next match is Japan vs Sweden on June 26 at 8:00 JST.

The study goal is not deep sports analysis. The goal is to talk naturally with coworkers or friends in Japan: show excitement, ask whether someone watched the match, and avoid sounding too overconfident.

Quick context in Japanese

日本はチュニジアに4対0で勝って、次はスウェーデン戦です。決勝トーナメント進出に向けて、かなりいい流れですね。

Japan beat Tunisia 4-0, and next is the Sweden match. They have pretty good momentum toward advancing to the knockout stage.

Grammar and Patterns

1. 〜そうですね: soft observation

突破できそうですね。

It looks like they can advance.

次の試合は難しくなりそうですね。

The next match looks like it might be tough.

2. 〜みたいですね: based on something you heard or read

日本は今、勝ち点4みたいですね。

It looks like Japan has 4 points now.

次はスウェーデン戦みたいですね。

Looks like the next match is against Sweden.

3. まだ分からないですけど: modest optimism

まだ分からないですけど、かなりチャンスはありそうですね。

We still do not know, but it seems like they have a pretty good chance.

Useful Phrases and Contrast Sets

  • 昨日の試合、見ました？ Did you watch yesterday's match?
  • 4対0はすごかったですね。 4-0 was amazing, wasn't it?
  • 日本、かなりいい感じですね。 Japan is looking really good.
  • 次のスウェーデン戦、大事ですね。 The next Sweden match is important.
  • このまま行ってほしいですね。 I hope they keep going like this.

Too Strong

日本は絶対勝ちます。

This sounds very confident. It can be okay with close friends, but it may feel too certain for workplace small talk.

More Natural

勝ってほしいですね。

I hope they win.

かなりチャンスはありそうですね。

It seems like they have a pretty good chance.

Mistake Review

Main correction target: avoid sounding too direct or overconfident when predicting sports results.

Less naturalBetterWhy
日本は絶対勝ちます。 勝ってほしいですね。 Sounds more modest and conversation-safe.
日本はスウェーデンより強いです。 スウェーデン戦も大事ですね。 Avoids sounding dismissive of the opponent.
突破します。 突破できそうですね。 Softens a firm claim into a natural observation.

Cultural Notes

In Japanese sports small talk, enthusiasm is natural, but modest phrasing is often safer. A good pattern is excitement plus caution:

  • 期待しちゃいますね。 I am getting my hopes up.
  • でも、油断はできないですね。 But they cannot let their guard down.

This balance sounds friendly, realistic, and workplace-safe.
